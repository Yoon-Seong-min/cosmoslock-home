üí° Core Technology Principle

IoT Device Mutual Verification Based on Communication History

Limitations of Existing IoT Authentication: All methods including passwords, certificates, and biometric authentication have a Single Point of Failure (SPOF). Once one authentication method is compromised, the entire system collapses.

NexAuth for IoT Innovation: Every IoT device in the network cross-verifies each other's historical communication records. New devices or abnormal access attempts are blocked by checking "Has this device had normal communication history with 70%+ of our network devices in the past?"

‚ö° Core Concept: 'Collective Intelligence Authentication' where the entire network becomes a witness
